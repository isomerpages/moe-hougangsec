<h4>Chingay Parade Volunteers</h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width:55%" height="auto" width="100%" src="/images/cpv1.jpeg">
</div>
<p>We would first like to thank Hougang Secondary School for being supportive
of the students volunteering for Chingay Parade 2017.</p>
<p>We are really grateful to have gotten the consistent support of the school
for the past few years to allow the students to join us in this massive
project and making it a successful one.</p>
<p>Secondly, we would like to thank the student volunteers who were involved.</p>
<p>All the student volunteers were committed, enthusiastic, stood strong
in their respective roles and gave their best to provide the best experience
for the audience.</p>
<p>With the support of different cohorts of students every year, we do hope
that each batch of them learned something out from this volunteering journey,
be it learning experience, making new friends or even a small gesture of
help to any fellow volunteer or audience. We hope it had been a fruitful
and memorable experience for them.</p>
<p>Do express our sincere thanks to this batch of students and share with
them that they had all done a brilliant job during the 2 parade days.</p>
<p>Lastly, we would like to thank the teachers' assistance, time and effort
spent on liaising and bringing the students down to the venue for the various
event dates. We really appreciate the effort to ensure the safety and welfare
of the students.</p>
<p>Once again, thank you!</p>
<p>Best regards,</p>

<h4>One Dream Journal</h4>
<p>One Dream Journal Launch at HS on 30 January</p>
<p>The Dream Journal is a collaborative project conceived by The One Dream
Initiative and motion, supportedby the SG50 Celebration Fund. The journals’
purpose is for Singaporeans, especially the youth to write, and draw their
dreams, hopes, aspirations and ambitions. The team behind this Dream Journal,
seeks to broaden dreams and inspire a deepening connection towards society.</p>
<p>The Dream Journal contains black and white images of students from Hougang
Secondary School, shot by Alecia Neo (an independent photographer and visual
artist). The creative team from Motion designed and carefully selected
inspirational quotes from well-known thinkers and history makers alike
and superimposed these quotes, on images of students and Singapore city
skyline.</p>
<p>The Dream Journal will be distributed to all secondary schools, ITEs in
Singapore. Indeed, HS is honoured to be part of this project.</p>
<p>“Borne out of One Dream’s raison d’être that everyone should have a Dream
--- a Dream which connects Self &amp; Society, that as one gets more successful
in life, to also help others fulfill their dreams. In a very simple way
should all citizens and residents think and behave this way, Singapore
and the world will be a much better, much brighter place.”</p>
<p>- Peter Ong, Leader, One Dream Initiative.</p>

<h4>HS Story - A Blanket of Love by Secondary Four Students</h4>
<p>A group of Hougang Secondary students, led by Ms Yen Nee and Ms Hwee Noi
visited one of the home hospice patients living in Hougang and presented
their handmade patchwork blanket to her.</p>
<p>For more information, please see the link here https://www.hca.org.sg/hcaconnect/a-blanket-of-love/</p>
