<h4><strong>Principal’s Message 2026</strong></h4>
<p>At Hougang Secondary School, we believe that every child can grow into
an empowered, future-ready individual with strong character and a clear
sense of purpose.</p>
<p>We aim to be an educational institution with a big heart and bold aspirations.
We are deeply committed to nurturing each Hougean to become not just a
future-ready learner, but a confident leader, a resilient innovator, and
a responsible community builder. Our work is guided by a simple yet powerful
belief: when young people are known, valued, and challenged meaningfully,
they rise.</p>
<p><strong>Nurturing Future-Ready Hougeans of Character and Purpose</strong>
</p>
<p>Our vision—Nurturing Empowered Future-Ready Hougeans of Character and
Purpose—anchors everything we do. In a rapidly changing world shaped by
technology, uncertainty, and new possibilities, academic excellence alone
is not enough. Our students must also develop the character, values, and
mindsets to navigate life with confidence and empathy.</p>
<p>At Hougang Secondary, character education is not an add-on. It is woven
intentionally into our curriculum, co-curricular programmes, and daily
interactions. Guided by our GREAT values of Gratitude, Respect, Empathy,
Adaptability, and Trustworthiness, we help students grow into grounded
individuals who care for others, contribute meaningfully, and act with
integrity.</p>
<p><strong>A Distinctive Focus on Leadership and STEM Innovation</strong>
</p>
<p>Hougang Secondary is proud to be known for our strong emphasis on community
youth leadership and applied STEM education, particularly in innovation.</p>
<p>Through structured leadership development opportunities, students learn
to lead with purpose—serving their peers, contributing to the community,
and making a positive difference beyond themselves. Our leadership programmes
nurture voice, responsibility, and confidence, empowering students to step
forward and lead with empathy and conviction.</p>
<p>At the same time, our applied STEM programmes expose students across all
streams to real-world problem-solving, design thinking, and emerging technologies.
From foundational coding and computational thinking to hands-on innovation
projects and industry-linked learning experiences, students learn to apply
knowledge meaningfully—not just to excel in examinations, but to solve
authentic problems and prepare for the future.</p>
<p><strong>Every Child Known, Every Child Growing</strong>
</p>
<p>As a school offering the full range of courses, we are deeply committed
to a holistic and inclusive education. We recognise that students come
with different strengths, starting points, and aspirations. Here in Hougang
Secondary, every child has the opportunity to grow and succeed.</p>
<p>Our teachers care deeply about their students. They believe in high expectations
coupled with strong support, and they work tirelessly to bring out the
best in every learner. Beyond academic development, we place strong emphasis
on student well-being, growth mindset, and positive relationships—because
young people thrive when they feel safe, supported, and inspired.</p>
<p><strong>A Strong Community, Meaningful Partnerships</strong>
</p>
<p>Education is a shared endeavour. We value strong partnerships with parents,
alumni, community organisations, and industry partners who journey alongside
us in shaping our students’ growth. Together, we create rich learning experiences
that extend beyond the classroom and connect learning to life.</p>
<p>Hougang Secondary is more than a school—it is a community where students
are encouraged to discover who they are, what they value, and how they
can contribute meaningfully to society.</p>
<p><strong>Looking Ahead</strong>
</p>
<p>As we look to the future, we remain steadfast in our commitment to nurturing
Hougeans who are confident in their abilities, secure in their values,
and ready to face the world with courage and purpose. We will strive to
be a school where every child is cared for, challenged, and empowered to
go forth and shine.</p>
<p><strong>Brandon Lee</strong>
</p>
<p>Principal
<br>Hougang Secondary School</p>
