<h3>Introduction</h3>
<p>Angklung/Kulintang and Gamelan Ensemble comprises of both Angklung and
Gamelan ensemble. Be amazed by the soothing sound these instruments bring
to one’s soul, uplifting one’s spirit! For truly, the joy of music is real.</p>
<h3>Vision</h3>
<p>To inspire others through the joy found in music&nbsp;</p>
<h3>Mission</h3>
<p>Building one’s character through music</p>
<h3>Motto</h3>
<p>The ensemble is only as strong as its weakest player</p>
