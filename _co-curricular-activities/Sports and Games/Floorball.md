<h3>Motto</h3>
<p>Play with Passion, Lead with Dedication, Be an Inspiration.</p>
<h3>VISION</h3>
<p>We aim to nurture all-rounded passionate, dedicated and inspiring floorballers
and future leaders in the game, school and community.</p>
<h3>MISSION</h3>
<p>To play with heart, mind and grit and be an inspiration to Hougeans.</p>
